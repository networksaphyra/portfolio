Who Am I?



Hello, I am Mohak Kochhar from Kitchener, Ontario and a current high school student at Cameron Heights C.I.

I created this personal website to showcase my skills, interests, hobbies, and to give you a glimpse into who I am.

Apart from Programming, I love to Play Chess

Chess has been a significant part of my life. I've achieved a peak rating of 2000 ELO on chess.com, which I'm quite proud of. I've participated in and won several chess tournaments at hackathons and local events. My next goal is to earn an official FIDE title, which I'm actively working towards.

Soccer provides a welcome physical balance to my tech-focused activities. While I play casually, I find it an excellent way to stay active and maintain friendships. The sport offers a refreshing break from coding and allows me to enjoy some friendly competition and teamwork in a different context.

Hackathons are more than just coding events for me; they're opportunities to connect with like-minded individuals. I enjoy the collaborative atmosphere, where I can meet new people, exchange ideas, and work on innovative projects. These events have been important in expanding my network within the tech community and have led to many valuable friendships and collaborations.
