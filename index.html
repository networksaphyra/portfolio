Who Am I?



Hi Everyone, I am Mohak Kochhar from Kitchener, Ontario.
I am a high school student at Cameron Height C.I.


Apart from programming, some other activities that I love to do!

Apart from Programming, I love to Play Chess

Chess has been a significant part of my life. I've achieved a peak rating of 2000 ELO on chess.com, which I'm quite proud of. I've participated in and won several chess tournaments at hackathons and local events. My next goal is to earn an official FIDE title, which I'm actively working towards. Chess continues to challenge me intellectually and strategically.

Soccer provides a welcome physical balance to my tech-focused activities. While I play casually, I find it an excellent way to stay active and maintain friendships. The sport offers a refreshing break from coding and allows me to enjoy some friendly competition and teamwork in a different context.

Hackathons are more than just coding events for me; they're opportunities to connect with like-minded individuals. I enjoy the collaborative atmosphere, where I can meet new people, exchange ideas, and work on innovative projects. These events have been instrumental in expanding my network within the tech community and have led to many valuable friendships and collaborations.
